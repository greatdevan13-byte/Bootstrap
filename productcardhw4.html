1.1. How do I connect the GlowMate Smart Lamp to my Wi-Fi network?
To connect your lamp, download the GlowMate app from the App Store or Google Play. Open the app, select “Add Device,” and follow the on-screen instructions to connect via your home Wi-Fi. Make sure your phone is connected to a 2.4GHz network during setup.
2. Can I control the lamp with voice commands?
Yes! GlowMate is compatible with Alexa, Google Assistant, and Siri. Once linked through the app, you can use commands like “Turn on GlowMate,” “Set lamp to blue,” or “Dim the light to 50%.”
3. What is the “Focus Mode” and how does it work?
3. What is the “Focus Mode” and how does it work? Focus Mode uses a preset lighting pattern designed to reduce eye strain and boost concentration. You can activate it through the app or by saying “Activate Focus Mode.” It gradually shifts between cool white and soft blue tones over a 90-minute cycle.
